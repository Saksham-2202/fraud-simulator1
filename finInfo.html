Government Financial Guidelines & Scam Awareness

This page is dedicated to helping you understand key financial safety information and avoid common scams. Stay alert, stay protected.

  • Never share your OTP, CVV, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone.
  • Do not click on suspicious or shortened links sent via SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
  • Always verify a caller's identity before providing financial information.
  • Check the legitimacy of apps before downloading. Avoid apps from unverified sources.
  • Secure your devices with strong passwords and antivirus protection.

üìå Things to Remember

  • Government officials, banks, or police never ask for your private banking details.
  • Check URLs carefully. Fraud websites often mimic official ones but have spelling errors.
  • Beware of fake KYC update calls. Always update KYC via official bank apps or branches only.
  • Report any scam attempt immediately to the authorities.
